Cinnamon Rolls

Ingredients

For the Cinnamon Rolls:

  • 1 packet of active dry rapid rise yeast
  • 1 cup warm non dairy milk
  • 3 tablespoons granulated sugar
  • 1/4 cup vegan butter, melted
  • 1/4 teaspoon salt
  • 2 1/2 cups 1:1 gluten gree flour
    • We recommend Bob's 1:1 Baking Flour

For the Filling:

  • 1/3 cup brown sugar
  • 2 tablespoons ground cinnamon
  • 4 tablespoons vegan butter, room temperature

For the Vegan Cream Cheese Frosting

  • 1 1/2 cups powdered sugar
  • 2 tablespoons vegan cream cheese
    • We found Miyokos gave the best consistency, but Daiya or Kite Hill also worked great!
  • 2 tablespoons vegan butter, room temperature
  • 1 teaspoon vanilla extract
  • Pinch of salt
  • 1 1/2 tablespoons non dairy milk

Directions

  1. Combine the milk, sugar, and yeast in a stand mixer fitted with the dough hook. Allow to sit for 5 minutes. The mixture should foam, signaling that the yeast is active.
  2. Add the melted butter, 1 cup of the flour, and the salt. Mix until just combined. Add the remaining flour and mix until the dough forms a ball and comes together. Turn the dough out onto a heavily floured surgace and knead for 15 minutes or until the dough is mostly smooth.
  3. Coat a large bowl with oil and place the dough into the bowl. Cover with plastic wrap and set in a warm area to proof for 1 hour.
  4. In a small bowl, combine the brown sugar and cinnamon for the filling. Lightly grease a 9 inch round baking pan. Set both aside.
  5. After 1 hour, the dough should be doubled in size. Turn the dough our onto a liberally floured work surgace. Roll the dough into a 9 x 13 inch rectangle. The dough will be thick. This is what you want. Spread the melted butter over the dough, coating it evenly all the way to the edge. Sprinkle on the brown sugar/cinnamon mixture and rub into the butter to comine.
  6. Working from the long edge, roll the dough into a log, pinching the ends so it stays together. Using a serrated knife or dental floss (even better!), cut the dough into 8 equal buns. Place into prepared pan.
  7. Allow the dough to rise a second time. Cover the pan with a cloth and let rest in a warm place for 1 hour.
  8. Preheat the oven to 375 degrees F. Bake for 20-30 minutes, until the cinnamon buns are golden brown on top.
  9. While the cinnamon rolls are baking, make the cream cheese frosting. Combine all frosting ingredients in a bowl and whisk until cobined.
  10. Once the cinnamon rolls are out of the oven, cool for 10 minutes. Microwave the frosting for 10-15 seconds to thin it out slightly, then spread over the warm cinnamon rolls and serve!
